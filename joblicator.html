01 / Product

An application system that stays yours.

Joblicator turns a scattered job search into one deliberate, reviewable workflow.

Maintain a structured profile, capture postings from URLs or pasted descriptions, and track each application from intake to final export.

A configurable three-stage Ollama pipeline extracts context, builds a targeted résumé, and writes a matching cover letter with verification loops.
