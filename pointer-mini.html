01 / Product

Every assessment artifact, connected.

Pointer-mini brings the fragmented parts of authorized security testing into one persistent workspace.

Define scope, authorization, testing windows, rate limits, restricted techniques, and granular authority before active work begins.

Captured traffic, tool output, hypotheses, evidence, findings, standards coverage, and reports remain connected and accessible outside the application.
